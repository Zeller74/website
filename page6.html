The Peacemaker The Peacemaker is a single shot hitscan that does 21-70 damage per hit. The revolver holds 6 shots and can be shot twice every second.


Fan The Hammer McCree can fan the Peacekeeper’s hammer that shoots 6 shots over .67 seconds. It does between 13.5 - 45 damage per hit.


Combat Roll McCree dives in the direction he’s moving, effortlessly reloading his Peacekeeper in the process. He moves 6 meters over .5 seconds. This ability has a cooldown of 8 seconds.


Flashbang McCree throws a blinding grenade that explodes shortly after it leaves his hand. The blast stuns enemies in a radius of 2 meters and stuns them for .7 seconds. This ability has a cooldown of 10 seconds.


Deadeye Deadeye is the ultimate McCree possesses. He builds up 250 damage over 1 second for enemies in his sight. While he is in this state, he is extremely vulnerable to attacks.
